Margin & Padding w/ "box-sizing: border-box;"

  • box-sizing: content-box;
  • This is default.
  • Because the border is 5px on every side, the width will be 10px more than 33.3% of the viewport.
  • box-sizing: border-box;
  • Padding and border are included in the width.
  • The width will be 33.3% of page.
